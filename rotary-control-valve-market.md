<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Rotary Control Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gate Valves</li><li>Diaphragm Valves</li><li>Ball Valves</li><li>Butterfly Valves</li><li>Plug Valves</li><li>Other Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary control valve market encompasses various types, each serving unique applications. Gate valves provide on/off control, operating with a wedge mechanism. Diaphragm valves use a flexible diaphragm to regulate flow, ideal for corrosive fluids. Ball valves feature a spherical closure element for quick shut-off. Butterfly valves employ a rotating disc to manage flow efficiently. Plug valves utilize a cylindrical or tapered plug for flow control. Additional valves in this segment include pinch valves and check valves, catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
