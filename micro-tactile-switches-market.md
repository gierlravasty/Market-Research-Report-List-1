<p><p>Micro tactile switches are classified into several types based on their mounting methods and applications. SMD (Surface Mount Device) types are designed for automated assembly on circuit boards, offering compactness and efficiency. Surface mount types, similar to SMD, are attached directly to the surface but may differ in design specifics. Snap-in types feature a mechanism that securely locks into place within a panel, ensuring durability. Other types include variations tailored for specific uses or environments, enhancing versatility in applications.</p></p>
