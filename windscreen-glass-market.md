<p><p>The windscreen glass market is categorized into three main types: front windscreen glass, rear windscreen glass, and side windscreen glass. Front windscreen glass, often laminated for safety, provides visibility and protection from external elements. Rear windscreen glass, usually made of tempered glass, ensures structural integrity and visibility from the rear. Side windscreen glass complements the overall design and safety of the vehicle, available in various forms, including fixed and sliding types, serving both aesthetic and functional purposes.</p></p>
