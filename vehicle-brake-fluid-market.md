<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Vehicle Brake Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li><li>DOT 5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle brake fluid market is primarily categorized into DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1 types, each with distinct properties. DOT 3 is glycol-based, suitable for conventional vehicles. DOT 4, also glycol-based but with a higher boiling point, is used in performance vehicles. DOT 5 is silicone-based, offering better moisture resistance, ideal for vehicles not requiring standard fluids. DOT 5.1 is glycol-based with performance enhancements, designed for high-temperature conditions and compatibility with other glycol fluids.</p></p>
