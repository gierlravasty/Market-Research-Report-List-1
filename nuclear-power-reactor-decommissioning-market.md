<p><p>The nuclear power reactor decommissioning market encompasses the dismantling and removal of nuclear facilities. It primarily includes Pressurized Water Reactor (PWR) decommissioning, where reactors using water under high pressure as a coolant are safely dismantled. Boiling Water Reactor (BWR) decommissioning involves reactors that use boiling water to generate steam. Additionally, other types include decommissioning of non-conventional reactors, such as gas-cooled or fast breeder reactors. Each type requires specialized processes to ensure safety and environmental compliance during the decommissioning phase.</p></p>
