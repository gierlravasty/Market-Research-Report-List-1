<p><strong>The Disposable Oxygen Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Into The Water Humidification Tube</li><li>Surface Humidification Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Disposable Oxygen Tube market is segmented into two primary types: Into The Water Humidification Tubes and Surface Humidification Tubes. Into The Water Humidification Tubes enhance oxygen delivery by passing the gas through a water reservoir, adding moisture directly to the oxygen. This method is beneficial for patients requiring high humidity levels. In contrast, Surface Humidification Tubes maintain humidity by utilizing a heating element or heat-moisture exchange system along the tube's surface, ensuring oxygen remains adequately humidified during transport.</p></p>
