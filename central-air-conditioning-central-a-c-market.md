<p><p>Central air conditioning systems effectively cool large spaces and are categorized mainly into two types: split systems and packaged units. Split systems consist of an indoor unit and an outdoor condenser, providing flexibility and quieter operation. They are ideal for homes where aesthetics and space are considerations. In contrast, packaged units house all components in a single outdoor unit, making installation simpler and requiring less indoor space. Both types cater to various residential and commercial cooling needs, each with unique advantages.</p></p>
