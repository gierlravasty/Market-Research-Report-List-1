<p><p>The area image sensor market includes various technologies, primarily CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) sensors. CMOS sensors are known for low power consumption, high-speed performance, and integration capabilities, making them popular in mobile devices and cameras. CCD sensors, on the other hand, excel in image quality and low light performance, often used in professional photography and scientific applications. Other types include specialized sensors like NMOS or EMCCD, catering to niche markets with specific requirements.</p></p>
