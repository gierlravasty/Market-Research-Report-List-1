<p><strong>The EAS Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hard Tag</li><li>Soft Tag</li><li>Deactivator or Detacher</li><li>Detection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>EAS (Electronic Article Surveillance) systems encompass various market types designed to prevent theft in retail settings. Hard tags are robust, reusable security devices attached to merchandise, while soft tags are thin, adhesive labels that can be discreetly placed on items. Deactivators or detachers are tools used at checkout to remove or disable tags, ensuring customers can leave with their purchases. Detection systems consist of electronic gates or antennas positioned at store exits to trigger alerts when undetected items pass through, enhancing security and loss prevention.</p></p>
