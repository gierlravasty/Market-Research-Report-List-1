<p><p>The high conductive silicone rubbers market encompasses various processing techniques, including compression molding, extrusion, and injection molding. Compression molding involves shaping rubber by applying heat and pressure within a mold, ideal for large-volume parts. Extrusion involves forcing the material through a die to create continuous profiles, suitable for seals and gaskets. Injection molding injects heated silicone into a mold for precise, complex shapes. Other methods may include casting or 3D printing, catering to specialized applications and innovative designs in the industry.</p></p>
