<p><p>The lower limb exoskeleton robot market can be divided into two main types based on power supply: power-supply and no-power markets. Power-supply exoskeletons utilize batteries or external energy sources to enhance mobility and assist users in walking or rehabilitation, providing active support and advanced functionalities. In contrast, no-power exoskeletons rely on passive mechanisms, using springs and joints without external power, offering basic support and stability for users with limited mobility while promoting natural movement without mechanical energy assistance.</p></p>
