<p><strong>The Particle-Size Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser Diffraction</li><li>Dynamic light scattering (DLS)</li><li>Image Analysis</li><li>Coulter Principle</li><li>Nanoparticle Tracking Analysis</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The particle-size analyzers market includes several types. Laser diffraction measures size based on diffraction patterns of laser light. Dynamic light scattering (DLS) assesses particle size through fluctuations in scattered light due to Brownian motion. Image analysis captures and analyzes particle shapes and sizes using optical techniques. The Coulter principle uses electrical resistance changes to determine particle sizes. Nanoparticle tracking analysis (NTA) tracks individual particles in suspension via Brownian motion. Other methods encompass various niche and advanced techniques for specific applications.</p></p>
