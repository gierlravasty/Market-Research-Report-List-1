<p><p>The Ytterbium Fiber market is categorized into two primary types: Single-Clad Fiber and Double-Clad Fiber. Single-Clad Fiber features a core surrounded by a single cladding layer, optimizing for simplicity and efficiency in laser applications. In contrast, Double-Clad Fiber incorporates an additional cladding layer, enhancing pump light absorption and output power, making it ideal for high-performance laser systems. Both types cater to various applications in telecommunications, medical devices, and industrial laser systems, offering distinct advantages based on specific requirements.</p></p>
