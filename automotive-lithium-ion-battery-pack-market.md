<p><ul><li>Ternary Lithium Battery</li><li>Lithium Iron Phosphate Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive lithium-ion battery pack market primarily features two types: ternary lithium batteries and lithium iron phosphate (LFP) batteries. Ternary lithium batteries, composed of nickel, cobalt, and manganese, offer high energy density and improved performance, making them suitable for applications requiring longer ranges. On the other hand, lithium iron phosphate batteries boast enhanced thermal stability and longevity, making them safer and more durable, albeit with lower energy density, making them ideal for applications prioritizing safety and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
