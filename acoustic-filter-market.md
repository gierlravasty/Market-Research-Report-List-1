<p><strong>The Acoustic Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SAW</li><li>BAW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acoustic filter market primarily consists of two types: Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) filters. SAW filters operate by using surface acoustic waves on a piezoelectric substrate, advantageous for lower frequency applications like wireless communications. BAW filters, on the other hand, utilize bulk acoustic waves through the entire substrate, ideal for high-frequency applications such as 4G and 5G networks. Both types are essential in managing signal quality and reducing interference in various electronic devices.</p></p>
