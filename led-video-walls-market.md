<p><p>The LED video walls market features two primary types: slatted LED displays and embedded LED displays. Slatted LED displays consist of multiple slats arranged to create an eye-catching visual effect, enhancing spaces like retail and event venues. In contrast, embedded LED displays are integrated into architectural elements, delivering seamless visuals directly within structures, such as walls or ceilings. Both types cater to diverse applications, including advertising, entertainment, and information dissemination, capitalizing on the growing demand for dynamic visual communication.</p></p>
