<p><p>The confocal displacement sensors market is primarily divided into two types: monochromatic and polychromatic confocal sensors. Monochromatic confocal sensors utilize a single wavelength light source, providing high precision and resolution for measurements, making them ideal for applications requiring detailed surface profiling. In contrast, polychromatic confocal sensors employ multiple wavelengths, enabling faster measurements and broader material compatibility, which enhances their versatility in various industrial settings. Both types are essential for precise distance measurements and surface inspection in diverse applications.</p></p>
