<p><p>The cardiac imaging equipment market encompasses several key technologies. X-ray equipment provides basic imaging for assessing heart conditions. Ultrasound equipment utilizes sound waves to create real-time images of the heart, aiding in functional assessment. CT equipment offers detailed cross-sectional images, helping diagnose coronary artery disease and other cardiac issues. MRI equipment provides high-resolution images and is particularly effective for evaluating cardiac structure and function, making it invaluable for comprehensive cardiac assessments. Each type plays a vital role in cardiac diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
