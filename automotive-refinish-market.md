<p><strong>The Automotive Refinish Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Borne</li><li>Water Borne</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive refinish market is categorized into several types, primarily solvent-borne, water-borne, and others. Solvent-borne coatings are traditional products that use organic solvents, providing durability and a wide color range but raising environmental concerns due to VOC emissions. Water-borne coatings, on the other hand, utilize water as a solvent, offering lower emissions and minimal environmental impact, while still delivering good performance. Other market types may include powder coatings and UV-curable coatings, which cater to niche applications and sustainable preferences.</p></p>
