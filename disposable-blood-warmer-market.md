<p><ul><li>Baged Disposable Blood Warmer</li><li>Columnar Disposable Blood Warmer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable blood warmer market is primarily segmented into two types: bagged and columnar disposable blood warmers. Bagged disposable blood warmers utilize a flexible pouch to warm blood products through thermal conduction, providing portability and ease of use in emergency settings. In contrast, columnar disposable blood warmers feature a rigid column structure that circulates warm water or air, ensuring consistent temperature control, making them suitable for larger volume transfusions in clinical environments. Both types aim to enhance patient safety and transfusion efficiency.</p></p>
