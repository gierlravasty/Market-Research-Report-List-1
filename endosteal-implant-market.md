<p><p>The endosteal implant market primarily features two types: titanium implants and zirconium implants. Titanium implants are widely used due to their excellent biocompatibility, mechanical strength, and ability to integrate with bone. They are preferred for their longevity and track record in dental and orthopedic applications. Zirconium implants, on the other hand, offer a more aesthetic solution with their tooth-like appearance and corrosion resistance, making them popular among patients seeking a more natural look. Both types cater to diverse patient needs in the dental and orthopedic sectors.</p></p>
