<p><strong>The Spray Polyurethane Foam Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-Cell</li><li>Closed-Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spray Polyurethane Foam market is primarily divided into two types: open-cell and closed-cell foam. Open-cell foam features a porous structure that traps air, providing excellent insulation and soundproofing while being lightweight and flexible. It's ideal for interior applications. Closed-cell foam, on the other hand, consists of tightly packed cells, making it denser and more rigid. This type offers superior thermal insulation and moisture resistance, making it suitable for both commercial and residential exterior applications.</p></p>
