<p><p>Fly laser marking machines consist of three main types: CO2, Fiber, and UV laser marking machines. CO2 laser marking machines are ideal for non-metallic materials like wood, glass, and plastics, delivering high-quality engraving. Fiber laser marking machines are suited for metals and offer high-speed marking with excellent precision, making them popular in industrial settings. UV laser marking machines utilize short wavelengths for marking sensitive materials, ensuring minimal heat impact and providing fine detailing on various substrates. Each type caters to specific application needs.</p></p>
