<p><p>The Voice Operated Switches market is categorized into two primary types based on delay duration: Long Delay and Short Delay switches. Long Delay switches remain activated for an extended period after detecting a voice command, allowing users to perform tasks without frequent reactivation. In contrast, Short Delay switches turn off quickly after activation, ideal for quick, intermittent tasks. Both types enhance user convenience, providing efficient control across various applications, including home automation, assistive technologies, and industrial settings.</p></p>
