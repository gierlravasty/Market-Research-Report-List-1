<p><p>Green mining market types are primarily categorized into surface and underground mining. Surface mining involves extracting minerals from near the earth's surface, often utilizing eco-friendly technologies to minimize environmental impact and restore ecosystems post-extraction. This method is typically more visible and has a larger footprint but can be managed sustainably. In contrast, underground mining occurs below the surface, focusing on minimizing surface disturbance and preserving land and wildlife. Both methods aim to balance resource extraction with ecological preservation and sustainable practices.</p></p>
